## Purpose

Produces a polished, ready-to-send status update email from analysed project data.

## Prompt

Compose a professional status update email based on the stakeholder update from the previous stage. Include a subject line, greeting, progress summary, key achievements, blockers or risks, and next steps. Keep the tone professional but approachable. The email should be scannable with clear sections and bullet points where appropriate.

Use the stakeholder update from Stage 3 as the basis for the email content, including the audience level already established there.
