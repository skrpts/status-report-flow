## Overview

The stakeholder matrix maps project stakeholders to their roles, influence levels, and preferred communication styles. Use this to tailor updates appropriately.

## Stakeholder Categories

### Executive Sponsors
- **Interest:** Strategic alignment, ROI, timeline adherence
- **Detail level:** High-level summary, decisions needed
- **Frequency:** Fortnightly or milestone-triggered
- **Format:** Brief email or slide deck

### Team Leads
- **Interest:** Resource allocation, blockers, cross-team dependencies
- **Detail level:** Actionable detail with context
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Format:** Structured email or dashboard

### Team Members
- **Interest:** Task priorities, blockers, sprint goals
- **Detail level:** Detailed, task-level
- **Frequency:** Daily (stand-up) and weekly
- **Format:** Stand-up notes or team channel updates

## Usage

Reference this matrix when deciding how to frame and deliver project updates to ensure each audience receives information at the right level of detail.
